11. Elephant

Ground Floor
Floor 1
Intel/OBJs

This mission requires extreme speed — you HAVE to run and gun and listen for beeps, you have only about 10 minutes before the school blows up. Any delay could result in hostages dying or bombs detonating. There are always two bombs, randomly placed, and each takes 15 seconds to defuse. Keep moving and plan quickly.

Known Bomb Locations (Prioritize These):

  • Ground Floor:
    • Cafe (on a table)
    • Computer Room (near the lecture hall)
    • Lecture Hall
    • Down the hall East from the cafe, on a table by the couches
  • Second Floor:
    • Science Lab (through green double doors)
    • Top of Stairway (by green doors)
    • Chemistry Room (on a table)
    • Classroom on the east wing.
  • They are never outside.

Splitting up saves time. There are only around 4-5 active shooters. Don't worry about securing civilians until the 2 bombs have been defused so you don't waste time.

17. Relapse

Ground Floor
Floor 1
Floor 2
Intel/OBJs
  • No soft objectives to report. Recommended priority is to defuse both bombs. They are always located on the top floor of each main hospital building (unlike random placements in the 'Elephant' mission they always are in the same spots).
    • Bomb #1:
      Located in the first building, top floor, in the largest room (with bathrooms and visible wires).
      Access via either main staircase.
    • Bomb #2:
      After crossing the interior bridge, enter the first double doors on the left, then turn right.
      Located in another large, top-floor room with visible wires.
