READY OR NOT Mission Tips and Maps

1. Thank You, Come Again

Click to view full size

All hostiles and objectives are located on a single floor.

  • Crystal Leighton: Found in the small room marked “STAFF ONLY,” located to the left of the starting area.
  • Store Manager: Located near the center, close to the cash register booth.
  • Downed Veteran: Found in the diner area to the right of the start, lying near the dining booths.

3. Twisted Nerve

Click to view full size
  • Left House (not fumigated):
    • Ground Floor: Simply clear it.
    • Top Floor: Contains meth evidence, cash, and a child.
  • Right House (fumigated 🎪):
    • Ground Floor: Meth evidence in a small room to the right of the main entrance.
    • Kitchen: Active meth lab.
    • Second Floor: Another meth lab down the hall.
    • Attic and Tunnel at the rear of map: Simply clear it.

4. The Spider

Click to view full size

No soft objective to complete or special evidence to report.

  • Market (start area): Simply clear it.
  • Ground Floor: Clear the area. Note: Brixley is often around the dressing room in the center.
  • Roof: Simply clear it.

*If civilians are hard to locate, check the homeless tent areas.
