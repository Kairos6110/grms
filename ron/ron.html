11. Elephant

Ground Floor
Floor 1

This mission requires extreme speed ‚Äî you HAVE to run and gun and listen for beeps, you have only about 10 minutes before the school blows up. Any delay could result in hostages dying or bombs detonating. There are always two bombs, randomly placed, and each takes 15 seconds to defuse. Keep moving and plan quickly.

Known Bomb Locations (Prioritize These):

  • Ground Floor:
    • Cafe (on a table)
    • Computer Room (near the lecture hall)
    • Lecture Hall
    • Down the hall East from the cafe, on a table by the couches
  • Second Floor:
    • Science Lab (through green double doors)
    • Top of Stairway (by green doors)
    • Chemistry Room (on a table)
    • Classroom on the east wing.
  • They are never outside.

Splitting up saves time. There are only around 4-5 active shooters. Don't worry about securing civilians until the 2 bombs have been defused so you don't waste time.
